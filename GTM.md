# Go-To-Market Plan

## Target User

Early-stage startup founders, finance leads, and operations managers at teams of 5 to 100 people. These teams adopt AI tools quickly but rarely have formal SaaS procurement.

## Positioning

SpendPilot AI helps teams find AI subscription and API waste before renewal. It is faster than a spreadsheet and more focused than generic SaaS spend tools.

## Acquisition Channels

- Founder communities where AI tool sprawl is common.
- LinkedIn posts with anonymized audit examples.
- Free audit landing page for lead capture.
- Partnerships with fractional CFOs and startup operations consultants.
- SEO pages targeting "ChatGPT Team cost optimization", "Cursor vs Copilot cost", and "AI SaaS spend audit".

## Activation

The first activation moment is seeing a monthly savings number. The second is sharing the public audit URL with a finance or founder stakeholder.

## Pricing Hypothesis

- Free self-serve audit.
- $29 one-time detailed report export.
- $99/month team plan for saved history and recurring checks.
- Consultant channel can sell audits at $250 to $500 per client.
