# Metrics

## Product Metrics

- Audit start rate.
- Audit completion rate.
- Average tools per audit.
- Median current monthly spend.
- Median estimated monthly savings.
- Share link creation rate.
- Lead capture conversion rate.
- Summary generation success rate.

## Business Metrics

- Visitor to lead conversion.
- Lead to paid conversion.
- Average revenue per account.
- Customer acquisition cost.
- Payback period.
- Churn for recurring team plan.

## Engineering Metrics

- Build success rate.
- API route error rate.
- Summary fallback rate.
- Firestore write failure rate.
- Email delivery success rate.
- Lighthouse mobile score.

## Current Instrumentation Gap

The MVP does not yet include analytics events. A production version should add privacy-conscious event tracking for audit start, audit complete, share created, and lead captured.
